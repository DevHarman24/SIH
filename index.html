Research Foundation

Our approach is grounded in academic research on AI, sustainable tourism, and cultural heritage promotion. Here are the key papers informing our project.

  • COMMUNITY BASED TOURISM

    This paper validates our core mission by demonstrating how tourism can be a catalyst for sustainable development and poverty reduction when it is centered around the local community.

  • GAMIFICATION OF CULTURE

    Supports our rewards system by highlighting gamification as an effective strategy for cultural preservation, engagement, and promoting local sustainable development.

  • AI-POWERED TRAVEL PLANNER

    Directly relates to our app's main feature, outlining the use of AI to create smart, personalized, and efficient itineraries that enhance the user-centric travel experience.

  • A ML-BASED ANALYSIS OF TOURISM RECOMMENDATION SYSTEMS

    Informs the technical foundation of our recommendation engine, focusing on the machine learning parameters needed to deliver relevant and insightful suggestions to travelers.
